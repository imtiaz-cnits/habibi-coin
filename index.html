ABOUT US

Habibi Token is the first Arabic meme project that will Launching on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network Its primary use case is to spread love and laughter while also raising funds for charity works, and users can also use the Habibi token to purchase Habibi NFTs to send to their loved ones as either an apology gift or a show of affection gift.

FEATURE

Explore the ultimate features of Habibi Token

NFT GIFTING

Habibi NFT is a one-of-a-kind digital art that habibis can gift to their loved ones. Habibi tokens will be used to purchase these unique NFTs, and habibis can send them to their lovers as an apology or appreciation gift. These NFTs are mystery boxes once revealed it shows a personalized letter and Habibi tokens. These NFTs have different tiers, each tier represent the depth of the apology or the amount of love.

CHARITY WORKS

One pillar of Habibi token is to donate part of the funds to charity work, mainly to refugee and war affected people. Habibis can donate and vote for various charitable causes. Habibi holders can also propose a verified charity event which Habibi holders can vote for it, then the required funds will be allocated. Through our charity platform other projects can also donate and show there love to the people in need.

COMMUNITY FOCUSED

Habibi token is a community-focused project that seeks to bring people together for a common goal. The Habibi team is committed to creating a positive impact on society and making a difference in people's lives. Habibi has three pillars, first to reward its holders with life changing money, to spread love through innovative NFT gifting system, and charity donation program that allow projects and holders to donate and vote.
